About Rendo Pauwels

I am a graphic and web designer based in Belgium with a human sciences background, and I love being creative with digital art. This website is my hub to collect my work and projects and share them with others. I love the things I do, and love to work on new projects I get in my hands. My creative mindset in combination with constantly pushing myself to create new exciting designs are what keeps me going and enjoying the things I make. User interfaces and user experience design are two things I enjoy to develop most.

In my spare time I am interested in gaming combined with roleplaying and developing character backgrounds in regards of the game's defined lore and rules. I'm a Star Wars fan and love anything sci-fi or space related. Apart from the geekiness, I also play golf and mountainbike in my spare time.
