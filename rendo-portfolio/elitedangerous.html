Elite: Dangerous Ship Commercials

Elite: Dangerous is a space adventure, trading, and combat simulation video game developed and published by Frontier Developments. Piloting a spaceship, the player explores a realistic 1:1 scale open world galaxy based on the real Milky Way. These videos create an immersive feeling of how spaceship commercials would be like in the game's set universe.
