About Us

Café, restaurant, bar

Our Story

Luni's Kitchen was founded 30 years ago by award winning chef Gaspard Luni. He has set the Lantillian sector on the gastronomical charts with its reasonable prices and high quality dishes. After his retirement, currently at the helm of the kitchen is his daughter Neyra Luni who has finetuned the restaurant's meals to be simple, delicious and affordable.
