Bakura Company is a crime syndicate based in the Outer Rim with a galaxy-wide reach. Its main focus lays with arms shipments, exotic spice dealing and other delicate shipments as a front business. The syndicate is founded by an ex military unit hailing from the Empire with it's Captain at the head of the syndicate, notoriously known as The Jackal. Bakura Company was founded with the belief that the Empire the unit used to fight for does not exist any longer. Using an array of networks and quasi-military tactics they have set foot in the underworld, making a name for themselves. Their main agenda remains unknown while Bakura Company's Imperial past is rumoured debatable.
